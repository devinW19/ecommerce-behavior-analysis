# ecommerce-behavior-analysis
Focus: Business Intelligence, SQL, and Dashboarding.

This project simulates a real-world business environment. Instead of just looking at a single table, you’ll analyze how customers interact with a platform, identifying who the "high-value" users are.

The Dataset: Use the UCI Machine Learning Repository: Online Retail Dataset or a similar Kaggle dataset.

Key Tasks:

RFM Analysis: Categorize customers based on Recency (how recently they bought), Frequency (how often they buy), and Monetary value (how much they spend).

Cohort Analysis: Track retention rates over time (e.g., "Of the customers who joined in January, how many were still active in June?").

Visualization: Create a professional dashboard in Tableau or Power BI (or use Python's Streamlit).
